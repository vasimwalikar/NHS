NHS Forum

NHS Forum, a registered trust, was founded by National High School alumni, with the aim of propagating the core values of NHS among students and to help the less privileged students in pursuing their education.NHS Forum has conducted several programs over the last three years benefitting a good number of deserving students. This has been possible with the whole hearted support of National Education Society and the well-meaning alumni of NHS. The Forum plans to expand its activities to benefit more and more people.

NHS Forum also conducts, from time to time, events to bring together the extended NHS family, namely NHS alumni, its teachers past and present and NES, with the intention of seeking their support and promoting their support, and thereby expand its base. All with the intention of reaching more people.
